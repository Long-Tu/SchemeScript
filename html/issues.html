Known Issues

Here is a list of known issues:

  • Structural editing is highly experimental and has most certainly a number of bugs.
  • Starting with Eclipse 3.1, the console pages behave differently. The cursor is not moved automatically at the end of the buffer. When a key is pressed, however, the cursor is moved at the end of the buffer. This has nothing to do with SchemeScript, it is a feature of Eclipse 3.1.
